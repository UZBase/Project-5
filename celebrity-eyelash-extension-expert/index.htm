Pioneer of Eyelash Extensions

Dionne Phillips – The Pioneer of Eyelash Extensions to Hollywood’s A-List!™

“Dionne put the LASH in LA!”

Dubbed the premier authority of celebrity lash extensions and faux lashes, Dionne Phillips has spent her entire career on the cutting edge of fashion. With a keen eye and an uncanny ability to deliver personalized results with warmth and passion, Dionne is known as the pioneer of custom lash extensions. With a passion for training and educating the next generation of beauty industry professionals, Dionne has paved the way for many artists and estheticians the world over. When she isn’t working in her state of the art studio in Beverly Hills, Dionne can be found celebrating fellow women in business and encouraging young entrepreneurs to follow their dreams.

Eyelash Extensions on Demand in Hollywood 

 Dionne loved the moment of magic when her clients were transformed, and the instant beauty boost her eyelash extensions gave them. Her lashes did more than frame their face and open their eyes, it gave her clients a bump of confidence that make them sparkle and lit them up from the inside. Best of all, her eyelash extensions were long lasting and natural looking, created from silk fibers rather than plastic or other synthetics, and applied according to the strictest standards with the highest quality products.

 

Eyes have a unique language of their own and helping eyes speak was Dionne’s special gift. In addition to a steady hand and careful eye, she took the time to listen to her clients, working with them to achieve the look they wanted, from dramatic red carpet looks to more subtle applications that could enhance a natural lifestyle. 

She was soon the go-to for the very best eyelash extensions in Los Angeles, California, and a beauty advisor to an A list clientele whose privacy she treasured. Word of her discretion and skills got around, and what started as a friend helping friends quickly was soon to change the world of fashion forever.

As a licensed Esthetician with a growing business and plans to expand, D’Lashes Luxury Lash Spa still offers a customer-centered experience, recreating for her guests the intimate and relaxing pampering that attracted her original following. A leader in the field, she teaches and empowers her clients to take care of their eyes and helps them fall back in love with themselves and their lashes.

After several successful years providing LA’s most beautiful and durable eyelashes. Dionne Phillips decided to venture out on her own and make D’Lashes a reality. While working with a famous Beverly Hills salon, Dionne serviced clients like Victoria Beckham, Sandra Oh, Nia Long, Viola Davis, Naomi Campbell, Paris Hilton, Renee Zellweger, Mary J. Blige and a host of others.

Dionne’s Lashes are custom individual eyelash extensions that are attached to your natural eyelashes, lasting up to 6-8 weeks.  While you relax, the procedure is painless and takes approximately 2 hours. Whatever your choice of look, giving your natural eyelashes a full, long glamorous look.

Mascara can be a thing of the past.  Safe, Hypoallergenic and waterproof.  Easy to maintain, you can easily workout, take a shower or even swim in them.  Saving your time and money on mascara and fake strip eyelashes.

About Dionne Phillips

Eyelash Extension Expert & Founder

“I Believe In Giving You The Hottest Lashes!”

Lash Out!, The Pioneer of Eyelash Extensions to Hollywood’s A-List, Dionne put the Lash in LA! Customized Eyelash Extension Expert and beauty influencer.

Hollywood A-List

Dionne Phillips has been dubbed the premier authority in celebrity eyelash extensions and faux lashes. She began her career servicing clients such as Victoria Beckham, Naomi Campbell, Renee Zellweger, Lindsay Lohan, Mary J. Blige and a host of others while working at a well-known, prestigious salon. After several successful years of providing LA’s most beautiful and durable eyelashes, Dionne decided to strike out on her own, launching D’Lashes in 2005.

The salon features state of the art amenities and the kind of pampering you would expect and deserve. Take your eyelashes from thin unnoticeable eyelashes to rich, full, beautiful ones.

Tucked away in a private, serene studio space in Beverly Hills, D’Lashes approaches lash design with a feminine, modern point of view. Dionne’s goal is to create eyelashes that embrace fashion with an effortless elegance and confidence. D’Lashes encapsulates a fresh take on glamour, while Dionne’s technique and meticulous attention to detail give women a “new lash on life.”

Dionne has garnered editorial acclaim for both her lash styles and expert advice for celebrities. A Louis Vuitton City Guide favorite, she has made numerous broadcast appearances as an eyelash expert having covered pre-Oscar and Emmy fashion segments for Extra TV, Access Hollywood and Good Morning America, and Hall Marks channel, “Home and Family TV.  She has also been featured on The Doctors and Style Network. Leading Hollywood ladies and fashion tastemakers such as Jenna Dewan Tatum, Rachel Zoe, Serena Williams, Christina Ricci, Sharon Stone and Christina Milian, among others, flock to Dionne for her services, while her work has appeared in top publications such as Allure, WWD Beauty, InStyle magazine, Redbook, Town & Country and Vogue.

In 2012, Dionne launched her own luxury product line including her signature eyelash kits, which provide her clients with the tools to get her signature red carpet-ready looks at home. Dionne continues to work and live in Los Angeles where she passionately helps women look and feel more glamorous one lash at a time.
